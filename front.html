Geography

Vác town is located about 35km north of budapest and can be accessed by water, road and railway. The town is located at the foot of Naszály Mountains. It has a total area of about 61.60 km 2. The topography is faily level.

Vác has a warm summers and very cold cloudy winters and its partly cloudy throughout the year.Cloud cover varies as May to October experiences clear skies while October to May experiencing cloudier skies. Summers lasts about three and a half months (May-September) with an avarage temperature range of 27°C-15°C, while winter lasts for about the same time(November-March)  with an avarage temperature range of -3.5°C- 2°C. Wettest seasons are from April to August with avarager precipitation of 32% while the remaining months have precipitation of less than 15%.  For more information on the weather click here .

The town is almost  a thousand years old. It has been a strategic location for settlement since time in memorial, because of its location near a river, a forest and a hill.
